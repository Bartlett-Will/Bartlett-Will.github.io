Cleaning for Cancer

I have been searching for ways to help in the battle against cancer anyway I can. Through out my life my grandparents, uncles, and father has struggled with Cancer diagnosis'. I lost my grandparents to cancer, but due to the amount of research and awareness for this terrible disease both my uncle and father beat cancer. Raising awareness is something I've been doing since High School, but now, together we can help fund cancer research. That is why Under Pressure Cleaning will be donating 15% of every job to the Canadian Cancer Society. Together we can make a difference, every nickel counts.

- Will Bartlett, Founder and Manager of Under Pressure Cleaning

About

Cleaning peoples homes for a fair price and for a good cause feels good, and what feels better are happy customers seeing their homes and yards in a new light.
